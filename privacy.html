Privacy Policy

AITool.icu is an AI tools directory designed to help users discover, compare, and save useful AI tools. This page explains what information may be processed when you use the site and how that information is handled.

We aim to keep this site simple and transparent. Ordinary browsing of the directory does not require you to create an account, and many convenience features are designed to work directly in your browser.

1. Information collected during normal use

Most visitors can browse AITool.icu without submitting personal information.

Browsing the directory

When you browse pages such as the directory, category pages, Top 100, and tool detail pages, the site is primarily used as a public information resource.

Locally saved preferences

Some features such as favorites, compare selections, saved stacks, or draft-style inputs may be stored locally in your browser to make the site more convenient to use.

No automatic account creation

The site does not require user registration for ordinary browsing, searching, filtering, or comparing tools.

Device-side storage

In the current version of the site, locally stored selections generally remain on your device unless a future connected backend is added.

2. Information you choose to submit

If you use forms or contact features, you may voluntarily provide information to us.

Contact requests

If you contact us directly, we may receive information such as your name, email address, and the content of your message.

Tool submissions

If you submit a tool for review, we may receive the details you enter, such as the product name, website, category, pricing, and your contact information.

Newsletter or subscription features

If a newsletter or subscription feature is enabled, any information you choose to submit may be processed for subscription, updates, or related communications.

Purpose of use

Information you submit may be used only for communication, review, support, service improvement, or related site operations.

3. How data may be used

We aim to use information in a limited and reasonable way.

Site functionality

Stored selections and preferences may be used to provide convenient features such as favorites, compare lists, or saved stacks.

Communication

If you contact us or submit a tool, we may use your submitted information to respond, clarify, review, or follow up on your request.

Improvement and maintenance

We may review usage patterns, submissions, or feedback to improve the quality, organization, and usefulness of the directory.

Service updates

If the site later adds analytics, backend storage, email providers, or other integrations, the way certain data is handled may expand accordingly.

4. Third-party services

The site may evolve to use third-party tools or infrastructure.

Hosting and delivery

The website may be hosted and delivered through third-party infrastructure providers that process standard web requests as part of normal site operation.

Future analytics

If analytics tools are added in the future, they may process technical and usage-related information to help us understand site performance and visitor behavior.

Future newsletter providers

If newsletter or email providers are connected later, subscription-related information may be handled by those services according to their own policies.

Future databases or forms

If the site later uses hosted forms, databases, or submission systems, submitted information may be stored and processed through those connected services.

5. Updates to this policy

The site may continue to evolve, and this page may be updated when needed.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in site functionality, data handling, third-party integrations, analytics, newsletter systems, submission workflows, or legal requirements. Continued use of the site after such updates may indicate acceptance of the revised policy.

6. Contact

If you have questions about privacy or data handling, please contact us.

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests, please use the contact page.
